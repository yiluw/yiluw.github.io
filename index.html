About

Hi, this is Yilu.

I am currently studying at DKFZ in Heidelberg. My research interest is genomics and related computational biology, because I find genomes intriguing and coding not that difficult these days.

I also (struggle to) write stories and spend most of my free time reading or listening to podcasts, almost exclusively in Chinese.

I like teaching very much, both biology and writing. I would be very happy to be offered teaching opportunities.

My goal is to work both happily and meaningfully.

Writing

I worked as a journalist and wrote feature reports for three years during my Bachelor's, which, though I was not fully aware then, has become the career I feel certain I don't want to give up.

Since moving to Germany, I have started constructing prose-like stories, though nothing has been published yet. My goal is to get better in both quality and quantity and share them in the future.

Of contemporary writers, I like Fei Ying (费滢) a lot, though that means I will have to spend the rest of my life waiting for her sporadic posts and always-on-their-way stories.
